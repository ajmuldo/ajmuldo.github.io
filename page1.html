  1. A Good Job and Fair Wages - All South Dakotans who want to work should have the right to a good job where we earn a fair return on our work and receive a wage that allows us to support ourselves and our familes. 
  2. Quality Health Care - All South Dakotans should have the Right to Quality Healthcare regardless of income, job status, or a pre-exsiting condition.
  3. A Safe Job - South Dakota is 46th in Workplace Safety. South Dakotans are twice as likely to be injured or killed at work than the rest of the country. South Dakotans should have the right to a Safe Job free from violence, injury, or harassment.
