Who is BJ Motley?



Bj became a worker at the age of 6, working along side his father and brothers in the Orange Groves of Florida. At 18 he relocated to Sioux Falls, SD to puruse and obtain a Bachelor's Degree in Health & Physical Education at The University of Sioux Falls. He has been a fighter for the working class ever since. He has served as Steward, Secretary Treasurer, and now serves as President of the United Food and Commercial Workers Local 304a which represents over 3,000 South Dakotans.
